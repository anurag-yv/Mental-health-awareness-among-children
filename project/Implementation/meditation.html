Simple Meditation Tips

  • Find a quiet and peaceful place to sit.
  • Close your eyes and take deep breaths.
  • Focus on your breath or a calming word.
  • Start with 5-10 minutes daily and increase gradually.
  • Don’t judge your thoughts—just observe them and let go.
